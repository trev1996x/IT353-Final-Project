Chocolate Chip Cookies

 Ingredients:

 Directions:
  • 1 cup butter, softened
  • 3/4 cup white sugar
  • 3/4 cup brown sugar, packed
  • 2 eggs, large
  • 2 tsp. vanilla
  • 2 1/4 cups flour
  • 1 tsp. baking soda
  • 1/2 tsp. salt
  • 1 cup semi-sweet choloate chips
  • 1 cup white colcolate chips
  1. Heat oven to 375°F.
  2. Stir flour, baking soda, and salt; set aside.
  3. In a large mixing bown, beat butter with sugars until creamy.
  4. Add vanilla and eggs, one at a time. Mix on low speed until incorporated.
  5. Gradually add the dry ingredients to the wet ingredients.
  6. Stir in the chocolate chips.
  7. Using a 1 tbsp. scoop, drop onto a lightly greased pan.
  8. Bake for 9 to 11 minutes, or until golden brown.
  9. Remove cookies from sheet and immediately transfer to a cooling rack.
  10. Enjoy!

Pumpkin Bread

 Ingredients:

 Directions:
  • 3 1/2 cups flour
  • 3 cups white sugar
  • 1 cup vegetable oil
  • 4 large eggs, lightly beaten
  • 1 can pumpkin puree (15 oz.)
  • 2 tsp. salt
  • 1 tsp. baking powder
  • 1 tsp. ground nutmeg
  • 1 tsp. ground allspice
  • 2 tsp. cinnamon
  • 1/2 tsp. ground cloves
  • 2/3 cup water
  1. Preheat oven to 350°F.
  2. Whisk together the sugar and oil in a large bowl until well combined. Whisk in the eggs and pumpkin until combined.
  3. Combine the flour, salt, baking soda, baking powder, nutmeg, allspice, cinnamon and cloves in a medium bowl and whisk until well combined.
  4. Add half the dry ingredients to the pumpkin mixture and stir to combine. Add half the water and stir to combine. Repeat with the remaining flour and water.
  5. Divide the batter between the prepared loaf pans. Bake until cake tester comes out clean and the loaves are golden brown, about 1 hour.
  6. Let the loaves cool in the pans on a wire rack for 10 minutes. Remove from the pans and let cool completely, about 1 hour.
  7. Ice the loaves with cream cheese frosting. Sprinkle a light amount of cinnamon on top for decoration.
  8. Enjoy!
