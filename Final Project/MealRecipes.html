Stir Fry

 Ingredients:

 Directions:
  • 1 red bell pepper
  • 1-2 crowns broccoli
  • 1/2 white onion
  • 1-2 carrots
  • 2-3 whole chicken breasts
  • Lite soy sauce
  • 2 tbsp. stir fry sauce
  • 1 tbsp. avocado oil
  • Garlic powder
  • 1 tbsp. sriracha
  • 1 tbsp. chili paste
  • Ginger
  1. Chop all of your vegetables into bit sized pieces. Set aside for later.
  2. Slice chicken breast into bite sized pieces, about 1 inch.
  3. Set your wok to high heat and cook chicken. Season chicken with chili paste and siriracha while cooking.
  4. Once chicken is fully cooked, set aside.
  5. Pour avocado oil into hot wok and fry veggies. About 3-5 minutes until preferred tenderness is reached.
  6. Once veggies are cooked, pour in stir fry sauce and soy sauce. Then stir in cooked chicken and mix well.
  7. Enjoy!

Tortellini Soup

 Ingredients:

 Directions:
  • 1 package cheese tortellini
  • 1 lb. italian sausage
  • 4 cups beef broth
  • 4 cups spinach or arugula
  • 1 can fire roasted tomatoes
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • Grated parmesan
  • Red pepper flakes
  • Garlic and onion powder
  • Oregano
  • Basil
  1. Cook sausage and then set aside.
  2. In a large pot, bring beef broth to a boil. Then add tortellini.
  3. Reduce heat to low and let the tortellini cook according to the package instructions.
  4. Add in onion, tomatoes, spinach or arugula, seasonings, grated parmesan, and cooked sausage.
  5. Enjoy!
